Input format:

.turb:
x y 1/-1 
(-1 indicates the substation, 1 indicates turbine)

.cbl: 
capacity price max_usage
(where max_usage is the the maximum number of times the cable can be used)

For more details, please refer to the paper:
M. Fischetti, D. Pisinger, Optimizing wind farm cable routing considering power losses, European Journal of
Operational Research (2017), http://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.ejor.2017.07.061

(If you use these data, remember to cite the paper)
